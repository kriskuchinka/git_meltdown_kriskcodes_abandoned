Meet Kris Kuchinka

Born in southern California, Kris moved to Portland, Oregon when he was four years old. Kris has lived in the Portland/Vancouver area for over two decades. Currently, Kris lives in Vancouver, WA with his wife and 3 cats.

Kris is working on building his portfolio and enhancing his coding skills. He looks forward to helping local non-profits with web development and design needs, but also is excited with the prospect of landing an internship with a company.

With the goal of being a life-long learner, Kris has a lot of projects and ideas on which he is focusing. His main goals in life is to develop as a human, husband, and coder.
